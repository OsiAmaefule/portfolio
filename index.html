    About Me

    Have you ever watched a Marvel movie and thought, what super power would I want, if possible? Some might say they want the ability to fly, while others might say invisibility. I, on the other hand, want the super power of computer programming. Programming is not only a super power, but an art. In my years of programming I've learned to not only converse with a computer, but solve problems in the most efficient way possible, with the added bonus of enhancement of the solution over time. Knowing that it is possible to create something out of nothing is the best super power to have. Who wouldn't want that?
